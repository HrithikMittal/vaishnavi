ANOSMIA

loss of smell

Anosmia is a disorder in which a person is unable to smell. It can be either permanent or temporary. Temporary situation is when the receptors are functional but due to some problem it is not able to recieve the signals. A permanent situation is when the receptors or the neurons are damaged.

It can be due to nasal blockages, inflammation of nasal passage or destruction of a temporal lobe. Both nostrils are seperately tested. It is done using books containing different smells in different intensities. Using this either one or both of the nostrils are completely or partially inefficient.

Anosmia is diagnosed with the CT scans (using x rays to create the image of the brain), MRI scans (uses radiowaves to create image of the brain), X rays etc.
